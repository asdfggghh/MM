The 4 Pillars of Smart-Shield

🩺
Digital Medical Profile
Instant access to blood group, medical history, and drug allergies (Penicillin, Sulfa, NSAIDs) in one scan.
📡
Auto-SOS Alert
Automatically sends the family a Live GPS location via SMS/WhatsApp alert upon scanning.
🏥
Smart Hospital Locator
Provides a GPS-based list of the nearest Trauma Centers and ICUs, including Hallet and Regency.
🧪
Blood Bank Sync
Connected to the e-RaktKosh API for real-time blood availability checks for life-saving transfusions.
